The Core Team

The foundation of Anika's Cabin and Events Place has always been family-centered which is to be expected from a family-run business.

Inspired by the owners' daughter Anika, whose gleefulness always grew whenever family was gathering in spaces that seeped out warmth and comfort, they envisioned creating a retreat house that could be a home away from home for every guest.
